<p><p>The ground deicing chemicals market is primarily divided into two types: liquid and solid. Solid deicing agents, commonly composed of sodium chloride or magnesium chloride, are often used for their cost-effectiveness and ease of storage, although they may act more slowly than liquids. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining safe surfaces during winter conditions.</p></p>
