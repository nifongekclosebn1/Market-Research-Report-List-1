<p><ul><li>Cathode Electrode Sheets</li><li>Anode Electrode Sheets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrode sheets market consists of two primary types: cathode and anode electrode sheets. Cathode electrode sheets, typically made from materials like lithium cobalt oxide, serve as the positive terminal in batteries, enabling energy storage and release. Anode electrode sheets, often composed of graphite or silicon, act as the negative terminal, facilitating ion movement during charging and discharging. Both types are essential in various applications, especially in lithium-ion batteries for electric vehicles and portable electronics, driving demand in the energy storage sector.</p></p>
