<p><p>The ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) sensor market is divided mainly into two types: film-based ITO sensors and glass-based ITO sensors. Film-based ITO sensors utilize thin films of ITO on flexible substrates, offering advantages like lightweight design and flexibility, making them suitable for applications in wearable devices and flexible displays. In contrast, glass-based ITO sensors are built on rigid glass substrates, providing robustness and stability, often used in touchscreens, displays, and other electronic devices requiring durable, transparent conductive materials.</p></p>
