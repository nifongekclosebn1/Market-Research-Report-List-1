<p><p>The passive magnetic bearing market is primarily divided into radial and other types. Radial type bearings support radial loads and maintain rotor stability using magnetic forces, requiring no external power for operation. They are commonly used in applications where maintenance and wear are concerns. Other market types include axial magnetic bearings, which manage axial loads, or hybrid systems that combine magnetic and mechanical elements. These variations cater to diverse industrial needs, enhancing efficiency and reducing friction in rotating systems.</p></p>
