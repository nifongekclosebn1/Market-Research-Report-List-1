<p><p>Hydraulic valve actuators are essential components in controlling fluid flow in various systems. The market can be segmented into linear actuators, which convert hydraulic pressure into straight-line motion to open or close valves, and rotary actuators, which utilize hydraulic force to produce rotational movement for valve operation. Linear actuators are commonly used in applications requiring precise positioning, while rotary actuators are favored for their ability to handle larger loads and provide a compact solution in tight spaces.</p></p>
