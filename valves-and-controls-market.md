<p><p>The valves and controls market includes various types designed for specific applications. Sliding-stem globe valves offer precise flow regulation, ideal for throttling services. V-notch ball valves feature a notched ball for enhanced flow control in applications requiring quarter-turn operation. Butterfly valves are compact and lightweight, suitable for quick shut-off. Angle valves change the direction of fluid flow and are often used in space-constrained environments. Additionally, "Others" encompass specialized valves like diaphragm or check valves, addressing diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
