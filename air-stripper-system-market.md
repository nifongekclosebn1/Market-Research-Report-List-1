<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air stripper system market is primarily divided into two types: sieve tray systems and packed tower systems. Sieve tray systems utilize trays to promote contact between air and water, enhancing mass transfer efficiency for volatile organic compounds removal. In contrast, packed tower systems employ packing materials to create a larger surface area for air-water interaction, facilitating effective stripping processes. Both systems are essential for treating contaminated water, and their selection depends on factors like efficiency, operational costs, and specific application requirements.</p></p>
