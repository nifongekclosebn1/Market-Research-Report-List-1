<p><strong>The Under display Fingerprint Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Fingerprint Module</li><li>Optical Fingerprint Module</li><li>Ultrasonic Fingerprint Module Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The under-display fingerprint module market encompasses three primary types: capacitive, optical, and ultrasonic fingerprint modules. Capacitive fingerprint modules utilize electrical currents to detect ridges and valleys on a finger, offering quick response times. Optical fingerprint modules capture images of fingerprints using light, providing high-resolution scans. Ultrasonic fingerprint modules employ sound waves to create detailed 3D maps of fingerprints, enhancing accuracy and security. Each type serves distinct consumer preferences, impacting their adoption in smartphones and other devices.</p></p>
