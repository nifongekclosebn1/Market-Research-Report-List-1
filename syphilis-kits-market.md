<p><p>The syphilis kits market comprises two main types: non-treponemal tests and treponemal tests. Non-treponemal tests, such as RPR and VDRL, detect nonspecific antibodies and are often used for initial screening. Treponemal tests, including FTA-ABS and EIA, identify specific antibodies against the Treponema pallidum bacterium and confirm syphilis infection. Both types are essential for diagnosis and monitoring of the disease, each serving unique roles in clinical settings for effective syphilis management and public health initiatives.</p></p>
