<p><p>The surgical electric staplers market is divided into two main types: disposable staplers and reusable staplers. Disposable staplers are designed for single use, ensuring sterility and minimizing the risk of cross-contamination during procedures. They are convenient and effective for various surgical applications. In contrast, reusable staplers are built for multiple uses, requiring sterilization between procedures. They often offer cost benefits in high-volume settings and can be more environmentally sustainable, but might involve additional maintenance and handling protocols.</p></p>
