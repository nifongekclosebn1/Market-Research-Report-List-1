<p><p>The Dual Emission X-Ray Absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment market is primarily segmented into central and peripheral DEXA bone densitometers. Central DEXA devices are primarily used in clinical settings to assess bone mineral density (BMD) in the lumbar spine and hip, providing precise diagnostics for osteoporosis. In contrast, peripheral DEXA devices are portable and focus on measuring BMD in extremities like the wrist or heel, offering a quick screening tool for osteoporosis risk. Each type serves distinct clinical applications and settings.</p></p>
