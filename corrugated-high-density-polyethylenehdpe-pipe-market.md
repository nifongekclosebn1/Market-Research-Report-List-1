<p><p>The Corrugated High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) Pipe market consists of two primary types: Single-Wall and Double-Wall Corrugated HDPE Pipes. Single-Wall HDPE Pipes are lightweight and flexible, primarily used for drainage and irrigation applications. In contrast, Double-Wall HDPE Pipes feature a dual structure with an outer corrugated layer for strength and an inner smooth wall for enhanced flow characteristics, making them ideal for larger-scale applications like stormwater management and sewage systems. Both types are valued for their durability and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
