<p><p>The lens driver IC market is categorized into single channel, dual channel, and four channel types based on the number of control channels for optical components. Single channel drivers control one lens, ideal for simpler applications. Dual channel drivers enable simultaneous control of two lenses, enhancing functionality for more advanced systems. Four channel drivers are designed for high-end applications, managing multiple lenses or motors for complex imaging tasks. This segmentation caters to varying performance needs across consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
